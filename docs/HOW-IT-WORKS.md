
# How It Works Documentation

![How It Works](../assets/img/how-it-works.svg)

This document details the structure and intent of the "How It Works" subpage.

## Overview
The "How It Works" page serves as an educational deep-dive into the methodology of the AlignSynch platform. It explains the "Soft Communication Tool" concept, breaks down the 5-step process, and defines the core cognitive framework (Feel, Wish, Think, Guess).

## Page Structure

### 1. Introduction ("What is it?")
- **Concept:** Defines AlignSynch as a "Communication Gizmo" and "Relationship Risk Management App".
- **Key Terms:** Initiator, Counterparty, Opinions, Aspects.

### 2. The 5-Step Process (Blue Section)
1. Pick a customizable Relationship Template.
2. Evaluate & Record your Feelings and Opinions.
3. Invite the Counterparty.
4. Compare & Discuss the Results.
5. Get in Synch!

### 3. Purpose & Methodology
- **Purpose:** Reach agreement, harmony, and understanding.
- **Simple Steps:** Visually highlights the flow between Initiator and Counterparty.
- **Relationship Recovery:** Uses the "Souffl√©" metaphor to explain how the tool helps when relationships collapse.

### 4. The Goal
A teal banner listing 4 key objectives:
1. Reach agreement.
2. Softly communicate desire for change.
3. Handle expectations.
4. Reduce stress.

### 5. The Four Opinions
Defines the core metrics used in the app:
- **Feel:** Your perspective of Now.
- **Wish:** Your perspective of Future.
- **Think:** Your perspective of Their Now.
- **Guess:** Your perspective of Their Wish.

### 6. Statistics Footer
Lists usage statistics (e.g., "52% To make a good Relationship a better fantastic Relationship").
