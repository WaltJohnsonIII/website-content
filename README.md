# AlignSynch Website Mockup and Content Registry

![AlignSynch Home](assets/img/home.svg)

**AlignSynch** is a comprehensive relationship management tool designed to help users align and synchronize expectations in both personal and business lives. It acts as a communication bridge to uncover gaps in understanding and foster better connections.

## Site Navigation & Structure

The application is organized into the following key sections, accessible via the main navigation:

- **Home**: The central landing page featuring the value proposition, methodology, and benefits.
- **Relationships**: A deep dive into the specific types of personal and professional connections supported by the platform.
- **How it Works**: A detailed guide on the "Express Opinions" and "Communicate" workflow.
- **Pricing**: Subscription models and tier information.
- **FAQ**: Answers to common questions about the "Alignment" process.
- **About Us**: Information about the mission to reduce relationship stress and surprises.

---

## Home Page Features

The index page acts as a complete introduction to the AlignSynch ecosystem:

### 1. Hero Section: "Align & Synch"
The entry point emphasizes the dual nature of the tool:
- **Business Life**: Targets Customers, Vendors, Staff, Managers, and Investors.
- **Personal Life**: Targets Spouses, Friends, Siblings, and Partners.
- **Core Concept**: Uncovering gaps in understandings and expectations.

### 2. The Communication "Gizmo"
A testimonial-driven section that positions the tool as a safe, non-confrontational way to ask difficult questions like:
- "Are you on the 'Same Page'?"
- "Where are your differences?"
- "Are you in Alignment?"

### 3. Relationship Types Matrix
A visual breakdown of the supported templates:
- **Personal**: Spouses, Dating, Roommates, Neighbors, etc.
- **Business**: Staff-Manager, Coworker-Colleague, Tenant-Landlord, etc.

### 4. Methodology: What does it do?
Explains the two-step process:
1.  **Express Opinions**: Users record opinions on specific "Aspects" of a relationship.
2.  **Communicate**: An Initiator invites a Counterparty to compare opinions, revealing "Gaps" to be discussed.

### 5. Pitfalls & Compromise
Addresses common cognitive distortions in relationships:
- Highlights the difference between how one *feels/wishes* versus the reality.
- Visualizes that solutions are often found in the "middle ground" rather than at the extremes.

### 6. Benefits Analysis
A comprehensive list of reasons to use the platform, including:
- Reducing risk of loss (divorce, business failure).
- Improving emotional well-being and physical health.
- Enhancing self-esteem and coping mechanisms.
